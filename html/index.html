We all love eating fresh veggies but maintianing a garden can be time consuming and difficult with limited space in an urban setting. Patio Gardening is a great alternative for those with little time or space, and serves as an accessible and easy way for us all to enjoy a green space with veggies, fruits and herbs! Browse around this Patio Gardening guide where you can find all sorts of helpful information.

Veggie of the Week

Cherry Tomato

Cherry tomatoes range in size from a thumbtip up to the size of a golf ball, and can range from being spherical to slightly oblong in shape. They are very easy to grow, require little care, and are great for beginners to get there garden started! Click on "More Info" to find out more about what is required to grow them and to find links to guides about how to grow them.
